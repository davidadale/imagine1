Song Contest

We know there are so many talented musicians out there, just waiting to be discovered, and we’d love to support you on your way to fame! Let us host your work as the “2012 One Family Theme Song.” All Imagine1.org contest winners will receive a $250 prize, while 2nd and 3rd place winners will be rewarded with One Family merchandise. The winner will be chosen from the first 25 submissions.

Requirements: Send video to YouTube\user\imagine1Tube and display lyrics as they play for international audiences to read. Band name and YouTube Channel link should appear somewhere.

  • Step 1- CREATE. Interpret the concept of One Family in the form of a song (as a video).
  • Step 2- SUBMIT. Email your final product or the YouTube link with SONG CONTEST as the subject to info@imagine1.org. Include your name, location, email address, and a few sentences explaining how your submission best represents the concept of One Family.
  • Step 3- VOTE. The One Family YouTube page (www.YouTube.com/user/Imagine1Tube) will be home to song video submissions, where you can vote for your favorites by “liking” the video.
