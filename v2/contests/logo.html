Logo Contest

We want you to design our imagine1.org logo! How would you describe One Family in a visual? It can be a literal or an abstract interpretation. Be creative and have fun with it! All Imagine1.org contest winners will receive a $250 prize, while 2nd and 3rd place winners will be rewarded with One Family merchandise. The winner will be chosen from the first 50 submissions.

Requirements: Logo must be a .jpeg image that can be easily recognized as a bumper sticker and understood worldwide.

  • Step 1- CREATE. Interpret the concept of One Family in visual format.
  • Step 2- SUBMIT. Email your final product with LOGO CONTEST as the subject to info@imagine1.org. Include your name, location, email address, and a few sentences explaining how your submission best represents the concept of One Family.
  • Step 3- VOTE. We will create a Facebook album of logos so the One Family community can vote by liking their favorite images. The link will be accessible at www.imagine1.org, as well.
