Vision

imagine1.org envisions a world where expanding circles of humanity come home to the concept of imagining then embracing one another as One Family.

Overview

Every human creation starts in our imagination so imagine1.org is here to help an expanding circle of people, just like you, imagine, then become a part of One Family. We want to create connections between you and people everywhere, aimed at inspiring all to see we are all the same at so many basic levels. We want to help everyone begin celebrating the potential for human unity. We want all to focus on what they share in common with others, to move beyond simply tolerating diversity, to adopt an entirely new mindset towards diversity, where we explore and embrace our differences. With a new attitude about unity, imagine1.org will help members join projects or events to build One Family and, most importantly, to take one step each day in their daily lives to embrace someone, perhaps someone they would not normally consider. At imagine1.org, we want to inspire you as much as you inspire us, creating a continuous cycle of goodwill within a growing family, our family, One Family.
