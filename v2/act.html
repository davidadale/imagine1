One Family Album

act [akt] to do something; exert energy or force.

While creating and engaging are ongoing acts, which we consider to be exceptionally consequential in your community and on imagine1.org, we urge you to take the next step! Once you feel ready to apply all your positive energy, it is time to act for the greater good!

Act now by joining cool projects all over the world and sharing moment of One Family love in your daily life. The categories on this page provide resources to “do,” “support,” and “join” consciously, with One Family in mind.

Do

  • Daily Challenge (motivate and connect with others by sharing and accomplishing daily challenges for good!)
  • KarmaTube (bringing inspirational stories to light using video to multiply acts of kindness, beauty, and generosity)

  • 43 Things (encourage fellow siblings in the “43 things” they’d like to achieve/accomplish, while also sharing your own goals)
