One Family Album

act [akt] to do something; exert energy or force.

While creating and engaging are ongoing acts, which we consider to be exceptionally consequential in your community and on imagine1.org, we urge you to take the next step! Once you feel ready to apply all your positive energy, it is time to act for the greater good!

Act now by joining cool projects all over the world and sharing moment of One Family love in your daily life. The categories on this page provide resources to “do,” “support,” and “join” consciously, with One Family in mind.

Do

  • Daily Challenge (motivate and connect with others by sharing and accomplishing daily challenges for good!)
  • KarmaTube (bringing inspirational stories to light using video to multiply acts of kindness, beauty, and generosity)
  • Global Oneness Project

    (films to inspire positive acts)
  • Do Something (join with others to Do Something, whether it's speaking your mind or finding clubs to join in your local area)
  • 43 Things (encourage fellow siblings in the “43 things” they’d like to achieve/accomplish, while also sharing your own goals)

Support

Projects

  • Kickstart (organization that helps impoverished nations create local businesses without worry of debt or failure)
  • Citizen Effect (a more personal approach to donating/funding projects worldwide)
  • Global Giving (a grassroots site that lets you search for and donate to your project of choice)
  • Plan USA (charity that works with communities to end poverty and create sustainability within impoverished nations)
  • One Family Inc. (organization driven to end homelessness)
  • City Year (give a year working as a tutor, mentor, and role model to help students and schools succeed)

Fair Trade

  • www.fairtradeusa.org (buy from fair trade sites, which ensure that people from all nations receive fair pay for their crafts/creations)
  • www.tenthousandvillages.com (a store that sells fair trade items)
  • www.sevenhopesunited.com (fair trade store)

Join

  • Oneness Project (focusing on the dances of universal peace)
  • Oneness (uses music and art as a tool to unite the human race)
