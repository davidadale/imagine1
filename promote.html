promote [pruh-moht] to help or encourage to exist or flourish; further: to promote world peace.

Now that you’ve taken the steps to imagine and be One Family, it’s time to expand your family! Find fun ways to get the message out, whether it’s by downloading business cards, writing your name and information on the back, and sharing them with people you meet or by spreading the word of imagine1.org at your school. Below is a list of ways you can start sharing, and we’d love for you to share your examples with us via email, the blog, and YouTube!

Create Your Own Way to Promote

  • Add imagine1.org to email signature line
  • Make stencils that say “imagine1.org One Family”
  • Design bumper stickers
  • Chalk “imagine1.org One Family” on sidewalks
  • Write “imagine1.org One Family” on your backpack or reusable shopping bags
  • Make imagine1.org T-shirts

Take Small Steps

  • Join us on Facebook
  • Subscribe to/like us on YouTube
  • Tweet about One Family
  • Email your friends and family telling them why you joined
  • Join the blog (coming soon)
  • Share daily acts of One!
  • Sign & share our One Family petition!

Leave a card with everyone you meet

This may be the most important way to build One Family. Print business cards and put a few in your wallet. Then as you meet people, talk to them about joining us and give them a card. The format allows you to print your name and email address. Please build our family.
