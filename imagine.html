imagine

One Family was born when many countries left their beautiful impression of imagine1.org's founder. Over a four decade period of his life, he visited 40 countries in every region of the world. He began to realize some important values should be adopted for humanity to live as one in a world where cultures, nations, and creeds collide. If you would like to hear more of his story, you can read the letter from him by clicking the link below, but more importantly read the letters of support coming from One Family members all over the world.

If you can imagine the world living as One Family, then take the next step and ask yourself, "Can I embrace the five values of One Family?".
