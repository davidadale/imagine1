embrace five values

One Family is a collection of people from any nation, creed, or culture who have adopted the five values shown below in their daily life.


  • 1
    I will embrace every nation, culture, and creed as having irreplaceable value.
  • 2
    I will focus on what I have in common with others before focusing on our differences.
  • 3
    I will explore the world by interacting with people who are different from me.
  • 4
    I will support all who unite our world and reject those who divide it.
  • 5
    I will encourage others to adopt these values and join One Family.

If one family values reflect your values, please take the next step and join One Family.
