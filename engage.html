Ready for a One Family reunion? We are, so we are creating ways to help.

First, we want to know about multicultural events in your area related to One Family. Over time, we will keep you updated on events happening in your country if you ask us to in your member profile, but please visit and help add to our calendar for now.

Next, we will create a blog. For now it is simply sharing your letters, but stay tuned for the real thing. Eventually it will have rooms you can help us create, maybe a library where we can talk about books, a kitchen where we can share old family recipes, or maybe a quiet room for meditations or consoling each other. If you have any ideas for making the blog, please send them to info@imagine1.org.

Sharing why we believe in One Family is the first step!

The central goal of imagine1.org is creating an opportunity for people all around the world to see that they want the same thing. They want to live as One Family. They want peace, prosperity, liberty, health, and purpose. We hope the letters from the original members of imagine1.org may inspire you to write your own or create some other way of expressing your support (YouTube, Facebook promotion). Please enjoy
